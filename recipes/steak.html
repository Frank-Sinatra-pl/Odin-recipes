Dry Steak

Description

This is one of my favourite things to eat

It has to be well done, with no blood in it, otherwise I won't eat it

Ingrediens

  • Beef
  • Side (rice or fries)
  • Your favourite Veg
  • Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the Steak (make sure its well done)
  2. As you frying meet, start cooking your side
  3. Same as in step 2, start preparing your Vegetables
  4. You should time everything together so nothing get cold
  5. Once all done and on the plate, put salt and pepper on
  6. Slap some sauce on the side
  7. Enjoy
